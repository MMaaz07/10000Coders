Shawarma


Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish of thinly sliced marinated meat, slow-roasted on a vertical rotisserie, served in pita or flatbread with sauces, vegetables, and pickles for rich flavor.



Hyderabadi Biryani


A fragrant rice dish from Hyderabad, blending basmati rice, tender meat, saffron, and spices, cooked in dum style, offering a rich, flavorful, and royal culinary experience.



Haleem


A slow-cooked delicacy made with wheat, lentils, meat, ghee, and spices, Haleem is creamy, protein-rich, and especially enjoyed during Ramadan for its hearty taste and nutrition.



Mutton Marag


A traditional Hyderabadi mutton soup, mildly spiced yet flavorful, prepared with tender meat, yogurt, and aromatic spices, often served as a starter at weddings and festive occasions.
